DESIGN

Phase 1
The purpose of this phase is to create the blueprint, which is detailed documentation of the business processes which will be implemented by Calfus​.

Entry Criteria

  • Environment Readiness
  • Pre-Built Questionnaire
  • Templates, MBP Flows

Input

  • Business Requirements Use cases
  • Data, Security Needs

Task

  • Functional & Technical Design Workshops for Business Process Mapping
  • Solution Design
  • Conversions & Integrations Strategy

Validation

  • Solution Governance Activities performed
  • Review findings are validated.
  • RTM is Validated
  • Final Inspection Review by the SQAR and Technical Lead Project /Phase Review by the SQAR​

Exit Criteria

  • Issue List​​
  • User Manuals​
  • Business / Test Scenarios, Design & Set- up Decisions & Reviews​
  • Gaps & Solutions​
  • Data Conversion Strategy​
  • Integration Strategy​
  • PaaS Components finalization

Output

  • Design Document sign off, Test Plan sign off.​
